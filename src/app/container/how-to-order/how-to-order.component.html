The Process

How to order

At Custom Merch we make the process of ordering custom garments as simple and straightforward as possible. Follow our simple guide and let us take care of the rest.

Select your garment

Choose from a wide range of blank garments by selecting your style, color, size, and quantity. Even if you're unsure, provide the garment type and approximate quantity for an initial quote. We offer various styles and price points through trusted suppliers and are happy to assist if needed.

Fill out our handy quote form

Submit your details using one of our quote forms. Choose the detailed form if you know the exact products, quantities, and sizes, or use the quick quote form for basic information.

Upload your artwork

Check our artwork guide for print file specs. We prefer Vector files (Illustrator, EPS, PDF) for best quality, but if you're unsure, send what you have and our designers will assist. We offer free basic design services, with complex work charged at a basic rate. Upload files up to 20MB via our quote forms; use WeTransfer for larger files.

What we do next

Once we have your artwork and all the details, one of our team members will get straight back to you.

Mockup

We provide a digital mockup of your order on your chosen garment, including color variants if needed.

Fast Quote

We offer a quick, competitive quote based on your details to reduce costs.

Sample

For a small fee, we can provide a blank or printed physical sample to qualify your approval.

Print

After approving the mockup and making payment, your order goes into production, typically within 1-5 working days.

Ship

After printing, we check and pack your order, then ship it via our trusted courier for delivery to your door.
